# Business Case Calculations

## Problem
WoodCorp has low on-time delivery leading to financial losses.

## Key Numbers
- Late Quantity: 15.8M KG
- Cost per KG: €0.75

## Current Loss
15.8M × 0.75 = €11.85M

## Improvement Scenario
Target OTD: 80%
Estimated reduction in late deliveries: 50%

## Potential Savings
€11.85M × 50% = ~€6M annually

## Conclusion
Improving delivery performance yields significant financial and customer satisfaction benefits.
